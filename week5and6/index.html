CORSA: a Provider of some of the best Software Defined Networking

Corsa Technology is a company leading the way in SDN (software defined networking) products and services. As they state on their site “SDN done right means you create economic saving and technological and operational simplicity for networking. You enable automatic and global orchestration of the network.” How they are able to lead the way in the market place is done with their brilliant ideas, and how they implement their technology.

When it comes to scale, Corsa will not leave their consumers wanting. The DP64xx data planes use multiple flow tables with a pure Openflow 1.3 implementation. Most of their impressive innovations are shown through reconfigurable datapath design. Because of this user networks can evolve with a base hardware platform that is flexible and microcode that can adjust to the user requirements.

Corsa data planes have embedded OVS based OF 1.3+ and OVSDB SDN controller interfaces and feature ultra-fast control plane communication. An internal X86 motherboard supplies superior processing power to remove any local flow modification bottlenecks. This makes it so that Corsa provides the only 100Gig capable OpenFlow 1.3 data plane in the marketplace. In the end because of their excellent product and dedication to service when it comes to WAN , you can not do any better then Corsa.
